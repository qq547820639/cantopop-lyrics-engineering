# 九大风格分支体系

## 风格一：慢歌钢琴叙事（参照《富士山下》《明年今日》）

**核心法则**：压抑×释放，物象触感化，动词暴力化，Bridge骤停裸声，尾奏一刀切。

**Style Prompt 模板**：
```
Cantonese male warm baritone piano ballad, 68 BPM unhurried breathing, urban melancholy, natural vocal grain, slightly breathy intimate, conversational phrasing behind beat, restrained storytelling, zero auto-tune, bridge all instruments cut to bare voice, final chorus voice cracks then falls to breath, ONLY felt piano soft pedal throughout, NO drums bass strings synths.
```

**适用词作**：《波子跌落》《暖柜》

---

## 风格二：Funky中板冷感（参照《人工智能》《听听》）

**核心法则**：律动欢快×人声冰冷，科技感物象，半讲半唱，Bridge骤停剩单脉冲音，全程不破音。

**Style Prompt 模板**：
```
Cantonese male vocal funky mid-tempo, 110 BPM, syncopated electric guitar strumming, walking bassline, tight crisp drums with rim clicks, acid-jazz inflection, warm baritone half-spoken half-sung, cold detached tone even when playful, funky groove but vocal is dead inside, bridge all instruments cut to single synth pulse and naked voice, NO big vocal belting, everything restrained and ironic.
```

**适用词作**：《双蓝剔》《嘟声》《31》

---

## 风格三：Band Sound撕裂摇滚（参照《浮夸》《六月飞霜》）

**核心法则**：耳语→嘶吼极限动态，物象破碎化，Bridge骤停剩反馈嗡声+玻璃碎，副歌3全破。

**Style Prompt 模板**：
```
Cantonese male rock ballad, 70 BPM verse building to explosive chorus, distorted electric guitar wall of sound, crashing drums, baritone voice from whisper to raw scream, natural vocal cracks, theatrical despair, bridge all instruments cut to guitar feedback hum and naked voice then glass shatter, final chorus vocal completely broken and collapsing, raw and bleeding and unpolished.
```

**适用词作**：《裂镜》

---

## 风格四：80年代Synth-pop舞曲（参照《重口味》《爱情陷阱》）

**核心法则**：138 BPM极速脉搏，谭咏麟式鼻音+机关枪咬字，Hook三连击，Bridge骤停念白后爆炸回归，尾奏突然cut。

**Style Prompt 模板**：
```
Cantonese male vocal, 1985 Hong Kong disco, 138 BPM, galloping octave synth bass, bright synth stabs, gated reverb snare, four-on-the-floor kick, machine-gun Cantonese delivery, Alan Tam nasal tone, bridge all instruments cut to heartbeat kick and whispered spoken confession then claps build to full band drop, sudden cold ending with silence, dancefloor frenzy with existential dread underneath.
```

**适用词作**：《收唔到掣》

---

## 风格五：电子实验（参照《热岛小夜曲》《人车志》）

**核心法则**：合成器冷感×Glitch故障美学，都市疏离，Bridge骤停剩数字脉冲+裸声，副歌3破音融入静态。

**Style Prompt 模板**：
```
Cantonese male vocal electronic experimental, 105 BPM, synth arpeggios and pulse beats, glitch effects and digital artifacts, baritone voice cold and detached, frequent falsetto transitions, urban alienation atmosphere, danceable but emotionally hollow, bridge all instruments cut to single digital pulse and naked voice, final chorus voice cracks then dissolves into static, NO acoustic instruments, ONLY synths and electronic textures.
```

**适用词作**：《房卡》《体温正常》《画面》

---

## 风格六：民谣叙事（参照《吟游诗人》《四季圈》）

**核心法则**：原声吉他为主，温暖叙事，轻微滞后于拍，Bridge骤停剩吉他单音+裸声，哲学式沉思。

**Style Prompt 模板**：
```
Cantonese male vocal folk ballad, 70 BPM unhurried storytelling, fingerpicked acoustic guitar, subtle piano touches, warm baritone with conversational delivery, slightly behind the beat, intimate and philosophical, no drums, only room ambience, bridge all instruments cut to bare voice and single piano note, final chorus voice falls to breath.
```

**适用词作**：《琴键》

---

## 风格七：暗黑电子/暗黑哥特（参照《黑择明》）

**核心法则**：低频暗色氛围，工业噪音，人声低沉气声，Bridge骤停剩心跳脉冲+裸声，副歌3破音被低频吞没。

**Style Prompt 模板**：
```
Cantonese male vocal dark electronic, 70 BPM brooding atmosphere, distorted synth pads and sub-bass drones, glitch artifacts and digital noise, industrial elevator hum, baritone voice low and breathy with occasional anguished whisper, urban alienation meets gothic dread, bridge all instruments cut to single heartbeat pulse and naked voice, final chorus voice cracks then swallowed by low-frequency rumble, NO acoustic instruments, ONLY electronic textures and dark ambient.
```

**适用词作**：《超载》

---

## 风格八：歌剧式管弦乐（参照《主旋律》《告别娑婆》）

**核心法则**：ABA三段式歌剧结构，弦乐团主奏，从呢喃到咏叹的极限动态，Bridge骤停剩大提琴独奏+裸声，副歌3破音被弦乐吞没。

**Style Prompt 模板**：
```
Cantonese male vocal orchestral ballad, 70 BPM with dramatic dynamic range, full string orchestra, grand piano, cinematic percussion, baritone voice from intimate whisper to operatic crescendo, theatrical staging feel, Hungarian cimbalom and glockenspiel accents, bridge all instruments cut to solo cello and naked voice, final chorus voice cracks then swallowed by swelling strings, tragic grandeur, NO electronic beats, ONLY orchestral instruments.
```

**适用词作**：《天桥》

---

## 风格九：R&B/Soul转音（参照《黑洞》《告别娑婆》）

**核心法则**：克制转音，宇宙感物象（黑洞、漩涡），Bridge骤停裸声。

**Style Prompt 模板**：
```
Cantonese male vocal soulful R&B ballad, 75 BPM, warm Rhodes electric piano, subtle brushed drums, soft bass, baritone voice with restrained melodic runs only in later choruses, breathy intimate verses, bridge all instruments cut to naked voice, cosmic loneliness atmosphere, NO showy vocal acrobatics.
```

**适用词作**：（待创作）

## 风格选择决策树

```
情感属性判断：
├─ 压抑内敛 → 慢歌钢琴叙事 (68 BPM)
├─ 冷感疏离 → Funky中板冷感 (110 BPM)
├─ 撕裂爆发 → Band Sound撕裂摇滚 (70 BPM)
├─ 上瘾失控 → Synth-pop舞曲 (138 BPM)
├─ 都市异化 → 电子实验 (105 BPM)
├─ 哲学沉思 → 民谣叙事 (70 BPM)
├─ 暗黑压迫 → 暗黑电子 (70 BPM)
├─ 悲壮宏大 → 歌剧式管弦乐 (70 BPM)
└─ 温柔克制 → R&B/Soul (75 BPM)
```
